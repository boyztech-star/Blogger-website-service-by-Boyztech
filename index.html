A custom domain is optional. I can set up your site on a free Blogspot subdomain, or connect a domain you've already purchased.

This service covers technical setup only — design, structure, pages and connections. Content writing can be discussed as a separate add-on.

Your site niche, logo or branding preferences, and access to the Google account you'll use for Blogger, Search Console, Analytics and AdSense.
